Project Proposal: NBA Fan Corner

Problem/Data Domain

Topic Overview

The NBA has a vast fan base seeking platforms to express opinions, connect with other enthusiasts, and stay updated with their favorite teams and players. However, there is a lack of centralized and interactive platforms that cater to these needs comprehensively.

Identify Information Technology Problem

The primary information technology problem is the absence of a user-centric, interactive platform for NBA fans. Existing solutions like the NBA's official app and Reddit's NBA subreddit offer partial solutions but lack personalization and interactive elements that enhance fan engagement.

Identify Existing Attempts

NBA's Official App

Provides news, scores, and video highlights but lacks interactive features like fan forums and prediction tracking.

Reddit's NBA Subreddit

Offers a platform for discussion but lacks personalization and official integration with NBA data.

App Description

Who are the users and what is the impact on minority groups

  • Current News

    Users looking for current news will have a place to congreggate quickly. Examples of these users are: night shift workers on their 5 minute break.

  • Proffessionals looking to be updated on the opinions of internet warriors

    Many proffessional pundits are engaged with the local fanbase. This can be the space where pundits and fans of the nba can interact, argue, and exchange opinions. This can create an entertaining atmosphere for the public and online figures.

  • Individuals looking for a fun discussion to kill time

    Many people just want a place to discuss the things they are interested in their past time. This app is the perfect place for that.

What will the users do with the app

  • Create individual accounts with specified team preferences.
  • Group conversations and and forums with other users.
  • Track statistics and performances of NBA players.

What kind of information will the users be viewing

  • NBA statistics and records.
  • Preferences and profiles created by other users.
  • Personalized dashboards and lists.

How will using this app help to solve your chosen problem?

In many sports, a hub of information is a crucial part of sports discussion. Meaningful sports discussions are not only built on a common home of information, but a centralized homr that many people can look to when discussing anything sports related. Right now, there is no centralized hub that serves as this domain for nba fans. This app can provide a community where nba fans can congregate and talk about anything nba.
